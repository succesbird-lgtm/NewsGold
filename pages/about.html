About NewsOnGold

A Financial Education Project — Mato Grosso do Sul, Brazil

Our Mission

NewsOnGold is a pilot educational project developed for final-year students in the state of Mato Grosso do Sul, Brazil. Our goal is to combine English language learning with practical financial education, preparing students for university and for the financial realities of adult life.

Students research, write, and publish one article every day — all supervised by a qualified teacher. Topics focus primarily on gold, precious metals, and related financial concepts, though we also cover broader financial literacy themes such as cryptocurrency, banking, investment, and economic history.

Why Gold?

Gold is one of the oldest and most universally understood stores of value in human history. It is a neutral, politically uncontroversial subject that students across all backgrounds find genuinely interesting. Studying gold provides a gateway to understanding currencies, inflation, investment, supply and demand, and global financial markets.

The Project Team

Articles on this website are written by final-year secondary school students, always under the close supervision of their English and Economics teachers. Each published piece is reviewed for accuracy, language quality, and educational value before being posted.

Monetisation & Sustainability

NewsOnGold is ad-supported through Google AdSense and similar platforms. Revenue generated by the website contributes towards the running costs of the project and, where possible, towards student activities such as the end-of-year celebration.

This model demonstrates to students how digital content can be monetised responsibly — itself an important lesson in financial education and digital entrepreneurship.
